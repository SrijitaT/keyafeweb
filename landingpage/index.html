about us

Our Journey

Birth of Keyafe

Keyafe was born on 16th December, 2019.

The idea behind

To imprint the touch of preservative-free, innovative & homemade bakery products.

Principles

What is unique at Keyafe??

Healthier, tastier & distinctive products

Them-based & Custom-made completely according to your desire.

What are we about??

We at keyafe have a vision to spread the idea of women empowerment & help the economically challenged by generating employment.

We deliver all over kolkata following proper safety protocols.

Goals

Short-Term Goals

To be able to cater to not only offline customers but also reach out to the netizen's tastebuds so that they can order online.

Long-Term Goals

To reach every foodie PAN India by introducing more outlets

To employ more women, help the poor & inspire to learn and earn more.
